Checklist for evaluating UI messages

This checklist will help you with creating UI messages of all types

Clarity:

  • Is the message easy to understand and free from jargon or technical language?

  • Is the use of words appropriate for the user’s technical level?

Tone and voice:

  • Does the message have unnecessary details?

  • Is the message concise but still informative?

  • Is the tone correct for the context?

Call to action (CTA):

  • Does the message guide users on what to do next or what to expect?

  • Are the buttons clear on what to do?

Accessibility:

  • Is the message visible at the right time and place?

  • Is the message readable and easy to scan for key information?
