Usefulness

Useful UI messages ensure that users receive clear, actionable guidance, helping them continue their journey within a product. A useful message explains the current status, provides clear instructions, and suggests appropriate actions to take.

Why usefulness matters

Useful UI messages help to:

  • Keep users engaged – helpful messages prevent frustration and keep users within the product or website.

  • Enhance clarity and security – users feel more confident when they understand what happened and what to do next.

  • Encourage action – well-written messages guide users toward the next step instead of leaving them stranded.

  • Create a positive experience – a supportive and instructional tone reassures users and makes interactions feel effortless.

How to write usefully

To write useful UI messages, it’s worth applying the following rules:

  • Explain the current situation and clearly state what happened.

  • Provide specific instructions and don’t forget to tell users what to do next.

  • Avoid overwhelming users with too many options.

  • Use a positive, reassuring tone to reduce frustration and encourage action.

  • Offer solutions. For example, suggest alternatives like links to other pages or ways to correct mistakes.

  • Keep it actionable and clear to ensure that users can quickly understand.
